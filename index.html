Why Embrace 2 Endure

The name was inspired from one of my favourite podcasts ‘The Fat Black’ and a big inspiration for this site. The podcast host, Kristian Manietta (my coach) drills into the listeners that we need to ‘Embrace the suck’. Embrace to Endure is a saying he keeps drilling his listener.

You need to make the choice to do the thing you don’t feel like doing, in the moment that you know you need to do it. We make thousands of choices every single day. These choices can build or break us. If we say we are going to DO something, even just to ourselves, and we DONT. That is a surefire way to shatter our confidence in ourselves. And by DO’ing you build momentum in doing versus coming up with excuses. One of the worst people to lie too is ourselves.

The ability to show up everyday, stick to the schedule, and do the work — especially when you don’t feel like it, is so valuable that it is literally all you need to become better 99% of the time. By choosing to endure, who choose to do it when we don’t feel like it.’ This is gold by Kristian. Go search him out, he is awesome.
