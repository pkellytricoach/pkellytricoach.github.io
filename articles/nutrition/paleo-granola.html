Paleo Granola

Ingredients
  • 2 cups Sliced Almonds
  • 2 cups Unsweetened Coconut Flakes
  • 1 cup Sunflower Seeds
  • 1 cup Pumpkin Seeds
  • 1/4 cup Honey
  • 1/3 cup Coconut Oil
  • 2 tsp Vanilla Extract
  • 1 tsp Ground Cinnamon
  • 1 tsp Ground Nutmeg

Mix all the ingredients with the exception of the coconut oil and honey in a bowl.

Microwave the coconut oil for 60 seconds. Then add the honey and microwave for a further 30 seconds

Add the honey/oil to the bowl of ingredients. Mix thoroughly with a spoon.

low heat in oven (circa 150 degrees) for 30 mins
